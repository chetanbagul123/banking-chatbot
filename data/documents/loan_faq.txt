Q: What documents are needed for a home loan?
A: Required documents: Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN), Address proof, Last 3 months salary slips, 6 months bank statement, Property documents, Form 16, Employment certificate.

Q: What is the current home loan interest rate?
A: Home loan interest rates start from 8.5% per annum for salaried individuals. Rates depend on your credit score, loan amount, and tenure. Higher credit score means lower interest rate.

Q: How long does loan approval take?
A: Personal loans: 24-48 hours. Home loans: 7-15 working days after document submission. Business loans: 3-7 working days.

Q: What is the maximum home loan amount?
A: Home loan up to 90% of property value for loans below Rs. 30 lakhs, 80% for loans between Rs. 30-75 lakhs, and 75% for loans above Rs. 75 lakhs.
